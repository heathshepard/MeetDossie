## Summary

Your template is in **good shape overall** — about 95% of the fields already follow clean `snake_case` and accurately describe what they capture. The audit found:

- **8 field renames recommended** — mostly inconsistent casing/punctuation, plus one truly mislabeled checkbox.
- **16 duplicate-name initials fields** — every "Buyer Initials P1" instance on pages 1-7 should be renumbered to its actual page (and corner) so Carter/Dossie can route initials per page deterministically.
- **1 functional bug to flag** — see §"Heads-up flag" at bottom.

Total clicks for the renames: about 24 fields × ~15 seconds each = **~6 minutes** of editor work.

## Heads-up flag — likely functional bug

The field at index 146 (`listing_broker_sellers_agent`) has these properties:
- **type:** `text`
- **size:** 0.0166 × 0.0135 (extremely small — checkbox-sized, not text-sized)
- **position:** x=0.5963, y=0.2510 on page 10

That's almost certainly meant to be a checkbox for the "Listing Broker has agreed to act as ___'s seller's agent or as intermediary" option — but it was created as a text field. When Heath fixes the name, also flip the `type` from `text` to `checkbox` in the DocuSeal editor. Otherwise Carter/Dossie can't render a checkmark there — only text — and the contract will look wrong.

There's a separate, properly-typed checkbox at index 154 (`listing_broker_seller_agent`) at x=0.5996, y=0.2313. So the template currently has TWO fields trying to do roughly the same job — one good (checkbox), one broken (text). You may want to delete index 146 entirely rather than rename it. Recommend you eyeball both in the editor before deciding.
