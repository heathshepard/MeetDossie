The part that hit me hardest: seeing in your CRM that leads closed with someone else. That's the worst kind of notification — finding out after it's already done.

I built Dossie because I was living exactly this. Drowning in lead volume from my team, trying to remember who needed what follow-up, then watching people buy houses they never told me they were looking at anymore.

What changed things for me was having something that watches for those buying signals before the close happens — not after. That surfaces "hey, this lead you haven't touched in 14 days just got active again" instead of "congratulations to them on their new home."

It also just drafts the follow-ups and sends them on a cadence I set, so high volume doesn't mean people slip through anymore.

You're not a bad agent. You're buried in logistics that shouldn't be manual.
