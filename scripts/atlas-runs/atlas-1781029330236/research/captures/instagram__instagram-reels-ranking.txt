How Instagram Feed Works
Instagram Feed is a place where you can share and connect with the people and things you care about. When you exit and reopen Instagram or refresh your feed, the photos and videos predicted to be most relevant to you will appear towards the top of your feed. In addition to seeing content from people you follow, you may also see suggested accounts that are relevant to your interests. Learn more about how Instagram Feed works.
