# Selfie Video Workflow — Heath's Founder-Led Short-Form

Heath records a vertical selfie video (30-60s). Pipeline produces multi-platform short-form ready for Zernio distribution.

## Tools
- **Phone** — vertical orientation, eye-level, mounted (car dash or shop tripod)
- **Submagic** ($16/mo) — auto-captions, auto-b-roll, multi-format export
- **Creatomate** (free tier) — Dossie intro/outro card overlay (optional, post-Submagic)
- **Zernio** — multi-platform distribution

## Recording rules
- Vertical 9:16, NOT horizontal
- Eye-level camera, NOT chin-shot from below
- Face the light source (window, sun), never have it behind you
- Phone 8-12 inches from mouth (mount close to dash, not arm's length)
- Car AC OFF, shop dust collector OFF — kills phone mic
- 2-3 takes back to back, don't rewatch — third take is usually strongest
- Outfit: dark solid color, button-up preferred (signals founder/expert)
- Background: wood shop (preferred — differentiated) or parked car

## End-to-end flow

1. **Heath records** raw selfie video (vertical .mp4 from phone)
2. **Heath sends** raw takes to Claudy via Telegram (multiple takes OK — Claude picks the best one)
3. **Claude reviews takes**, picks strongest, sends back to Heath with note ("use take 2")
4. **Heath opens Submagic app**, uploads chosen take
5. **In Submagic**: tap Auto Captions → choose "Bold" or "Spotlight" template → tap Auto B-Roll → export 9:16
6. **Heath sends Submagic export** back to Claudy via Telegram
7. **Claude** (a) optionally overlays Dossie intro/outro via Creatomate, (b) uploads to Zernio with platform-specific captions for TikTok / IG Reels / FB feed / LinkedIn / YT Shorts
8. **DossieMarketingBot** sends to Heath's Telegram for one-tap approval before posting

## Script formula that converts

Pain → math → reveal → scarcity → CTA. Example:
> "I pay my transaction coordinator $400 a deal. Last month I closed eight. That's $3,200. And I'm STILL the one waking up at 3 a.m. wondering if she sent the option fee receipt. That's why I built Dossie — an AI transaction coordinator for Texas real estate. $29/month locked forever for the first 50 founding members. 9 spots taken so far. Texas agents — meetdossie.com/founding."

- 30-45 seconds at natural pace
- Specific numbers (specificity converts)
- Founder's actual story (not generic)
- Scarcity hook (founding-spot counter)
- Clear single CTA (one URL)

## Don't ship if
- Audio has obvious background noise (AC, tools)
- Light is behind Heath (silhouette)
- Anything reads as "scripted" — if you can hear him reading, redo it
- The numbers aren't true (algorithm punishes inauthenticity)

## Cadence target
3 videos per week. Tuesday / Thursday / Saturday. Batch-record 6 at a time when possible (one shooting session, draft 6 scripts in advance).
