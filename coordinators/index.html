What Dossie Does

Your AI-powered TC assistant.

Dossie doesn't replace you — she handles the repetitive work so you can focus on the deals that need your expertise.

📬
Automated Follow-Up Sequences
Dossie sends timely, professional follow-ups to lenders, title companies, and agents on your behalf — with your name on them. No more chasing. No more manual emails.
⚡
TREC Deadline Engine
Every critical deadline is auto-calculated from the contract effective date. Option period, financing, title commitment, survey, closing — tracked and alerted automatically.
☀️
Morning Brief for Every Client
Each morning, get a clear summary of every active transaction — what's urgent, what's on track, and what needs your attention. One view. Total clarity.
📊
Multi-Transaction Pipeline
Manage all your clients' deals in one dashboard. See every transaction, every stage, every outstanding task — without juggling spreadsheets or sticky notes.
🎙️
Chat-Based Contract Entry
Describe a new deal in plain language and Dossie fills in the details, calculates the deadlines, and sets up the checklist. No manual data entry.
👁️
Reply Monitoring
Dossie watches your email threads and flags when a lender or title company hasn't responded. No more manually checking if someone got back to you.
