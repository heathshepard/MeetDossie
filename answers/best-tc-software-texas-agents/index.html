What 'best' means here

'Best TC software' is too vague to answer without context. Most Texas agents are juggling three different needs:

  1. Compliance and document storage — broker-required filing, audit trails, e-signature.
  2. Operational automation — deadline tracking, follow-up emails, document QA.
  3. Client-facing experience — milestone updates, closed-deal packages, viral artifacts.

Most products focus on one or two of these. The best stack for most Texas agents is a compliance platform (often provided by the brokerage) plus an automation layer on top.

The major options

Skyslope

Mature compliance and document-management platform widely used by mid-to-large brokerages. Strong on broker audit trails, document retention, e-signature, and team workflows. Less strong on TREC-specific deadline automation — deadlines are entered manually rather than computed from the contract.

Dotloop

Similar category to Skyslope — compliance, e-signature, document workflow. Strong UI for team collaboration. Like Skyslope, it doesn't deeply parse TREC contracts to compute deadlines.

Brokermint

Brokerage management platform with TC features. Strong on commission tracking and brokerage-level reporting. The TC layer is functional but generic across states.

Open To Close

TC-focused workflow tool with templating for repetitive transactions. Used by many freelance TCs.
