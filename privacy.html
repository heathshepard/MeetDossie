Privacy Policy

Last updated April 25, 2026

The short version. We collect only the information needed to run the Service, store your contracts and dossiers in encrypted cloud storage, and never sell your data. You can export or delete your data anytime by emailing us.

1. Who we are

This Privacy Policy describes how Dossie AI ("Dossie", "we", "us") collects and uses information when you use the Dossie websites and workspace at meetdossie.com (the "Service"). If you have questions, email heath@meetdossie.com.

2. Information we collect

  • Account information. Name, email address, and (when applicable) brokerage or role, provided when you sign up.
  • Authentication. Sessions and tokens issued by our authentication provider (Supabase). We do not store your password — Supabase handles password hashing.
  • Transaction data you enter. Property details, contract dates, party names and contact information, deal notes, checklist progress, and email drafts you create inside the workspace.
  • Documents you upload. Contracts, addenda, and supporting files. These are stored in our cloud storage and accessible only to your account.
  • Payment information. If you subscribe, billing is handled by Stripe. We receive your subscription status and the last four digits of your card from Stripe — we do not store full card numbers.
  • Usage and diagnostic data. IP address, browser type, timestamps, and basic event logs used to operate the Service, prevent abuse, and debug issues.

3. How we use information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To process payments and manage your subscription.
  • To send transactional emails (sign-in links, billing receipts, important account notices). We do not send marketing emails without your consent.
  • To respond to support requests.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

4. AI processing

Several features of the Service use third-party AI models (including Anthropic's Claude) to extract data from contracts you upload, draft messages, and summarize deal status. When you invoke one of these features, the relevant content (such as the text of a contract) is sent to the AI provider for processing under their data-processing terms. We do not allow these providers to use your content to train their models, and the providers we use do not retain your data beyond the time needed to return a response.

5. Where your data is stored

Account data, deal data, and document files are stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL and Supabase Storage). Supabase data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Payment records are stored by Stripe. Service logs may pass through our hosting provider (Vercel).

6. We do not sell your data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or the contents of your dossiers. We do not share your data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

7. Sharing limited to operators

We share information only with service providers who help us run the Service, under contracts that require them to handle data confidentially: our authentication and database provider (Supabase), our hosting provider (Vercel), our payment processor (Stripe), and the AI providers described above. We may also disclose information when required by law (subpoena, court order) or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Dossie, our users, or the public.

8. Your rights

  • Access and export. You can request a copy of the data associated with your account by emailing heath@meetdossie.com.
  • Correction. Most data fields are editable directly in the workspace; for anything you can't edit yourself, email us.
  • Deletion. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time. We will delete or anonymize your data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain records for legal or accounting reasons.
  • Marketing opt-out. Any marketing emails we send (if you've opted in) include an unsubscribe link.

9. California (CCPA) and EU (GDPR) residents

If you are a California resident, you have the right under the California Consumer Privacy Act to request access to or deletion of personal information we hold about you, and to opt out of any "sale" of your personal information — though as noted above, we do not sell personal information. If you are in the EEA or UK, you have rights under the GDPR/UK GDPR including access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and objection. To exercise any of these rights, email heath@meetdossie.com and we will respond within the timeframes required by applicable law.

10. Data retention

We retain account and deal data for as long as your account is active. After cancellation we keep your data for up to 30 days to allow reactivation, then permanently delete it (except limited records required for tax, billing, or legal purposes).

11. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards including TLS in transit, encrypted storage at rest, scoped database access via row-level security, and least-privilege service-role keys held only on our servers. No system is perfectly secure — please use a strong, unique password and notify us immediately at heath@meetdossie.com if you believe your account has been compromised.

12. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 18 and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced by email or in the workspace at least 14 days before they take effect. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

14. Contact

Privacy questions or requests? Email us at heath@meetdossie.com.
